What is Global Warming?

cc: Depositphotos/Anastasia_Panchenko

Global warming is the slow cooking of the Earth using what are called greenhouse gasses. There are five main greenhouse gasses: water vapor, carbon dioxide, methane, nitrous oxide, fluorinated gases. These gasses slowly warm the planet. Global warming will be the end of our planet if nothing is done about it. It is a common misconception that the planet will only be worse off for future generations. The truth is that we will all be doomed if this current trend continues. We are actively shortening the lifespan of our planet by doing nothing. Action is required if we want to maintain an inhabitable planet. Humans are notorious for letting our problems pile up, whether it be over-population the global food crisis, a pandemic, or over-mining. We can still change this planet, and the change begins with us.
